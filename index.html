What is Halvorsen?

Halvorsen is a personal course organizer built for students who want to stay organized throughout their semester — without the chaos.

Track your courses

Add all your courses in one place — instructor, credit hours, schedule, and personal notes all organized neatly.

Never miss a deadline

Log deadlines for every course and see them all at a glance, sorted by urgency so nothing slips through.

Built for students

No bloat, no accounts, no noise. Just a clean focused tool that helps you stay organized through the semester.
